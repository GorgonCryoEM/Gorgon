Detailed Description

Cholesky decomposition of a real symmetric or complex Hermitian matrix, A. A is assumed symmetric if real and Hermitian if complex. The lower triangular part of A is the only part accessed.

factor

The lower triangular factor in the factorization A = L . L^t (A = L . L^h if A is complex).

void  solve (Matrix *)

solve

Back solve the equation A . X = B, for symmetric (Hermitian) A, by the sequence:

Matrix A(n,n), B(n), X; X = B; CholeskyDecomposition L(A); L.solve(&X);
